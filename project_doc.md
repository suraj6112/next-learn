## Goal

The final website should feel similar in quality and user engagement to premium wedding, luxury event, and high-end entertainment brands, maximizing user engagement, watch time, trust, and inquiry conversion rates.


## High Engagement Strategy

The website should be designed not only to showcase services but also to maximize user engagement, increase session duration, and improve lead conversion rates.

### Instagram Reels Style Video Experience

A dedicated section on the Home Page featuring short vertical videos similar to Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts.

Features:

* Vertical video cards
* Auto-play when visible
* Swipe navigation on mobile
* Smooth transitions
* Full-screen viewing option
* "Watch More" button linking to Gallery

Purpose:
Wedding and event-related videos are the most engaging content format and significantly increase user retention and inquiry rates.

---

### Cinematic Hero Experience

The Home Page Hero Section should immediately create a premium first impression.

Features:

* Full-screen cinematic background video
* Slow zoom and parallax effects
* Elegant animated text reveals
* Premium gold glow CTA buttons
* Smooth scroll indicator

The hero section should feel like a luxury wedding film rather than a traditional business website.

---

### Premium Gold & Black Luxury Theme

The entire website should follow a luxury event-industry aesthetic.

Theme:

* Midnight Black Background
* Rich Gold Highlights (#D4AF37)
* Glassmorphism Components
* Elegant Typography
* Soft Glow Effects
* Premium Card Designs

This premium visual identity helps build trust and positions the brand as a high-end event service provider.

### User Retention Goal

The website should encourage visitors to:

* Watch multiple videos
* Explore galleries
* Browse event categories
* Read testimonials
* Submit inquiries

The overall experience should feel modern, cinematic, interactive, and premium, increasing both average session duration and lead generation.
